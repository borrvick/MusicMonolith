First Timer?
Don't worry, we got you covered.

Search

Promoting a rock song? Great, try typing in "rock". Is your song the next heartbreak hit? Fanastic! Try searching "broken heart". Type in a genre, mood, adjective, or what ever you think describes your song. There are playlists for everything. Let's find them!

Play

Does a playlist named "90's Rap Revisited" sound like the type that would enjoy your death-core song? Maybe not. Play a few songs in the playlist to see if your song would be a great addition. If it is, let's get to the fun part!

Pitch

There are over 60,000 tracks uploaded to Spotify every day. Playlisters are busy people and any time saved is appreciated. Want the upper hand? Do the work for them! Describe why your song fits perfectly with their playlist. You may even want to mention a specific track included in the playlist that your song would pair well with. What are you waiting for? Let's get to work!
