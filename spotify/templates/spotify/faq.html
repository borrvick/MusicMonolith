How does MusicMonolith Work?

MusicMonolith connects directly to Spotify and searches for playlists relevant to your search criteria. Once playlists are found, a list is displayed along with methods of contacting the playlist curator. Wanna keep track of all the playlists and curators you find along the way? Download the CSV file in your recent searches found in your account. Feeling fancy?...You can directly upload the CSV file to Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets for a hassle free spreadsheet format.

Why didn't the instagram button work?

Sometimes the instagram handle that the user provided has changed or the AI that finds it was tricked into thinking it was something else.

Why do I need an account?

We require an account because future MusicMonolith integrations may be depenant on Spotify credentials and permissions. Don't worry, this just means we may implement cool features like "Create Me a Playlist Based off of Songs Similiar to Mine". Pretty neat huh?

I forgot my password...is that bad?

Not at all! Just click the little button that says "forgot password?" when you are signing in.Ta-dah! You got your account back.
